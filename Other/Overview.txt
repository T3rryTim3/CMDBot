Main goal: Act as an automated user in discord; send messages, respond to people, and simulate other actions a user might take.

The bot will follow this timeline which depicts which actions should be taken.
Timeline:
    - Scan guilds and their channels.
    - Find channels of priority (Ones with the bot's username, or replies to it's messages)

Channel priority list (Top is most important):
    - Channel with the bot's name in messages
    - Channel with multiple recent messages
    - Channels with a question
